About Lorem Ipsum:

Lorem Ipsum is dummy text used in design, printing, and typesetting to visualize layouts before final content is available. It has been the industry's standard placeholder text since the 1500s.

When to use Lorem Ipsum:

  • Web design mockups and wireframes
  • Print layouts and typography testing
  • UI/UX prototypes before content is ready
  • When focusing on design elements without being distracted by readable content

All processing is done in your browser - your data never leaves your device.
