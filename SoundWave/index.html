Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the battery last?

The SoundWave Pro offers 40 hours of continuous playback with noise cancellation on, and up to 60 hours with it turned off.

Are the ear cushions replaceable?

Yes, all SoundWave models come with replaceable cushions that can be ordered through our website.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, our free app for iOS and Android allows you to customize EQ settings, control noise cancellation levels, and install firmware updates.

What's the warranty coverage?

SoundWave Lite comes with 1 year, Pro with 2 years, and Elite with 3 years of warranty covering manufacturing defects.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.
